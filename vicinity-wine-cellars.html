Wine cellars

Winemaking has for many centuries been firmly linked with South Moravia. This is due to the region's suitable soil and favourable climate. The vineyards of Moravia are divided into four sub-regions: Znojmo, Mikulov, Velke Pavlovice and Slovácky. These sub-regions are further divided into wine villages and vineyards.

With the onset of a market economy Moravian winemakers began to focus not only on quantity but also on quality. Therefore, today we can choose from a wide range of high-quality attributive wines produced both by small and large wineries.
